# Trainee quickstart

Run one virtual-patient case from sign-in to debrief in about ten minutes.
This is the shortest happy path; each step links to the full guide for
detail, options and troubleshooting.

::: warning Training only
The simulated patient is not real and the simulator does not provide medical
advice. Nothing you do here affects a real person.
:::

## Before you start

You need an account (ask your Teacher or use **Create Account**) and a
modern browser — Chrome or Edge if you want voice. Full prerequisites:
[Getting started](/trainee/getting-started).

## 1. Sign in

Open the simulator, enter your **Username** and **Password**, click
**Sign In**. Full detail and account creation:
[Getting started → Sign in](/trainee/getting-started).

## 2. Pick a case

If your Teacher set a default case it loads automatically. Otherwise open
**Settings → Open Settings → Cases** and open a case. Full detail:
[Getting started → Pick a case](/trainee/getting-started). Not sure which
case to run? See the
[acute-cases walkthrough](/tutorials/walkthrough-acute-cases).

## 3. Take a short history

You start in the **Patient** room. **Your first message starts the
session** — there is no separate start button.

1. Type `Hello, what brought you in today?` and press **Enter**.
2. Ask two or three focused follow-ups (onset, character, associated
   symptoms).

Full detail, open-question technique and other characters in the room:
[Taking a history](/trainee/history).

## 4. Order one lab

1. Click **Laboratory** in the bottom navigation bar.
2. In the catalogue (left), select one test relevant to your working
   diagnosis — or use a quick **panel** button (CBC, BMP, …).
3. Place the order. It appears in the worklist on the right.
4. When the worklist row turns **Ready**, click it to read the report.

Turnaround, panels, the results badge and the report viewer:
[Ordering labs & imaging](/trainee/investigations).

## 5. Give one treatment

1. Back on the **Patient** screen, click **Treatments** in the floating
   strip above the bottom bar.
2. Select a treatment, set **Dose**, **Route** and **Frequency**, and
   submit.
3. Read any contraindication or high-alert warning, then click
   **Administer** to start its effect.
4. Watch the [monitor](/trainee/vitals) respond over the next few ticks.

Dosing, warnings and administer/discontinue:
[Treatments & medications](/trainee/treatments).

## 6. End and debrief

1. On the **Patient** screen, click **End & Debrief** (top-right) and
   confirm.

   ::: danger Ending a case is final
   Confirming stops the timeline and locks chat, exams and orders. You
   cannot reopen an ended case.
   :::

2. You land in the **Consultant** room. Click **Start debrief** and talk
   through your reasoning.

Ending vs. leaving, transcript and notes: [Debrief](/trainee/debrief).
