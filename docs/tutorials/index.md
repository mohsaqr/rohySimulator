# Tutorials & media

Fast paths to a first success, plus a teaching walkthrough and the media
production standards. Every page here is a **condensed happy path** — it
points into the full guides for anything beyond the shortest route.

::: warning Training only
The simulated patient is not real and the simulator does not provide medical
advice. Nothing in these tutorials is clinical guidance.
:::

## Quickstart vs. full guide

A **quickstart** is the shortest sequence of steps that gets you one working
result — log in and finish a case, stand up a class, bring the app up
locally. It deliberately skips options, edge cases and "why".

A **full guide** (under [Using the Simulator](/trainee/),
[Teaching with Rohy](/educator/) and
[Running Rohy in Production](/operator/)) covers the same ground with every
option, fallback and constraint. Each numbered step in a quickstart links to
the guide page that explains it in full — read the quickstart to move, read
the guide to understand.

## First success in ~10 minutes

Pick the path that matches your role:

| You are a… | Path | What you get |
|---|---|---|
