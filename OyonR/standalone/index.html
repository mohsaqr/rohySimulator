What this estimates

Visible facial expression patterns from a cropped face — not the student's true internal emotion. Treat outputs as uncertain analytics signals, not ground truth.

Privacy

Inference runs locally in your browser. The demo does not store video frames, raw images, or audio. Only aggregate predictions are kept in memory until you close the tab.

Valence & arousal

Valence is roughly negative-to-positive affect. Arousal is low-to-high activation. Only multi-task models output them; expression-only models leave them blank.

Use limits

Do not use these outputs for grading, ranking, discipline, or automated judgments. Under EU AI Act Art. 5, emotion inference in education contexts is restricted. Treat this sidecar as research telemetry only.
